Mujaddid Alf al-Thani’s Maktubat (Epistles)


A detailed Study of the Maktūbāt(letters) of Imām al-Rabbānī Aḥmad al-Fārūqī al-Sirhindī (RA)
Course Instructor: Shaykh Sayed Farūq al-Hassanī (HA)
fiber_manual_record Ongoing
language English
group Open for Men & Women
date_range Every Sunday: 2:30pm - 3:30pm (UK Time)
mic Live & Recorded Classes
speaker_notes Q&A Sessions
This course presents a systematic exploration of the influential letters of the Sufi saint and scholar Shaykh Ahmad al-Faruqi al-Sirhindi رَحِمَهُ ٱللَّٰهُ, commonly known as Mujadid Alf al-Thani (Reviver of the Second Millennium).
These letters are pivotal works in the science of Tazkiyah and Ihsan. In’sha’Allah students participating in this programme will not only deepen their understanding of Islam & Tasawwuf but also connect with the profound spiritual and mystical insights of a renowned Wali of Allah ﷻ. This course hopes to enrich one’s spiritual journey, offering practical wisdom and inspiration for both personal growth in one’s connection with Allah ﷻ and a greater appreciation of the authentic Sufi tradition within the Islamic scholarly heritage, especially in relation to the Naqshbandi Tariqah.
Start Date: 15 Sha'bān 1445 - 25/02/2024
